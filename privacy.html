Privacy Policy & Terms

Last updated: January 12, 2026

I. Privacy Policy

WuJi (hereinafter referred to as "the App") values your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Data Collection & Usage

Local Storage: Using the SwiftData framework, all item names, prices, quantities, notes, and images you enter are stored locally on your device. We do not upload this data to any third-party servers.

iCloud Sync: The App enables Apple's iCloud service. Your data is synced to your private iCloud database via Apple's encrypted channels. The developer cannot access your iCloud container, nor view or modify your data.

2. Permissions

iCloud: Used to sync your inventory across multiple iOS devices.

3. Third-Party Services

The App contains no third-party advertisement SDKs or data analysis tools. We do not track your behavior.

II. Terms of Service

1. Software Usage

WuJi provides a tool for managing physical and digital assets. You own all the data you input.

2. Disclaimer

Data Loss: While we provide iCloud sync, the App is not liable for data loss caused by system environments or user errors. We recommend regular backups via the "Export" feature.
