Last updated: January 12, 2026

I. Privacy Policy

WuJi ("the App") is a minimalist asset management tool. We value your privacy and are committed to ensuring that your data remains under your absolute control.

1. Data Collection & Usage

No Data Collection: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All user-generated content is stored locally or in your private cloud container.

  • Local Storage: All item names, categories, and notes are stored on your device using Apple's SwiftData. We have no access to this local database.
  • iCloud Synchronization: Data is synced via Apple's CloudKit to your private iCloud container. This process is encrypted by Apple, and the developer cannot view your data.

2. Log Data

The App does not use any third-party analytics or tracking SDKs. Any crash reports we receive through App Store Connect are anonymized and used strictly for improving App stability.

3. Children’s Privacy (Age 4+)

WuJi is rated 4+ on the App Store. Because the App does not require account registration and does not collect any data, it is inherently safe for children. We do not track, request, or share information from any users, including children under 13. All data remains on the device under the user's or guardian's control.

4. Your Rights

You have full control over your data. You can delete your records at any time by removing items within the App or by deleting the App itself.

II. Terms of Service

1. Acceptance of Terms

By using WuJi, you agree to these terms. This App is provided as a personal organization tool for managing your assets.

2. Changes to This Terms and Service

We may update our Terms and Service from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Terms and Service on this page.

These terms and service are effective as of 2026-01-12.

3. Disclaimer

The Service is provided "AS IS". We are not responsible for any data loss resulting from hardware failure or iCloud synchronization issues. We strongly recommend using the "Export" feature to create manual backups periodically.
